About Us

Gleam started in January 2016 as a student-led team participating in the Zahn Innovation Center’s Annual Venture Competition. It is a secure, HIPAA compliant web platform that enables therapists to provide more targeted and efficient therapy and makes it easier for their patients to practice their coping skills between sessions. Gleam works to improve therapeutic relationships, reduce frustrations, and ease the path to patient recovery.

Our ultimate mission is to make the fight against mental illness easier. That’s why we are teaming up with clinicians to remove the obstacles they face in treatment!
